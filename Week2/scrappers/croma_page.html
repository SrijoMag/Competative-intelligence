      Overview

      Immersive Active Noise Cancellation

      Featuring 32dB, these boAt Nirvana Ion ANC Pro Truly Wireless Earbuds block out unwanted background sounds for an uninterrupted listening experience. So, whether you’re on a busy train, working in a cafe, or travelling by flight, you can stay fully immersed in your favourite playlists or podcasts without distractions.


      Hi-Res LDAC Sound

      Powered by LDAC technology, these ANC earbuds deliver studio-grade hi-res sound with three times more data transmission than standard Bluetooth audio. Hence, you experience every beat, note, and vocal detail just as the artist intended, which is ideal for audiophiles and sound purists.


      Low Latency BEAST Mode

      Designed with BEAST Mode, these truly wireless earbuds provide ultra-low latency for lag-free gaming and streaming. So, whether you’re in the middle of a competitive match or watching action-packed videos, the sound syncs perfectly with the visuals for a smoother experience.


      Unmatched 120-Hour Playback

      Delivering up to 120 hours of total playtime, these truly wireless earbuds ensure extended music sessions and seamless calls without frequent recharging. So, from long workdays to weekend trips, enjoy uninterrupted performance with a single charge.


      Dual EQ Modes

      Equipped with Dual EQ sound modes, these truly wireless earbuds allow you to switch effortlessly between balanced audio for everyday listening and boosted bass for energetic workouts. Furthermore, this makes them perfect for fitness sessions, gaming, or relaxing at home with your favourite tracks.


      Sweat-Resistant Design

      Built with an IPX4 water-resistant design, these truly wireless earbuds stand up to sweat and light splashes, making them ideal for workouts, runs, and outdoor adventures. As a result, you can enjoy your music confidently, no matter where your day takes you.


      Stylish Finish

      Crafted in an elegant crystal finish, these boAt earbuds combine premium looks with ergonomic comfort. Furthermore, the lightweight build ensures a snug fit, allowing you to wear them for long hours, from office meetings to evening commutes, without any discomfort.
